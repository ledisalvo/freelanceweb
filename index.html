Intro

Hi! Let me introduce myself — I'm a software developer. I started my journey in 2010 working with Silverlight and AngularJS. Throughout the years, I've constantly sought to improve my skills and expand my tech stack.
During the early days of quarantine, I launched a personal project: streaming on Twitch while coding a website and teaching programming (back then, Python). I also explored new programming languages live — everything was streamed in real time.
The project gained traction, reaching an audience of over 100 viewers per stream. However, in August 2020, my wife got pregnant, and in May 2021, I became a father. Naturally, I paused the project — priorities matter.
Even while it was on hold, I kept learning and working on personal projects using React. That's why today, I proudly consider myself a full stack developer.
I’m always open to new challenges — if you're looking for a reliable, experienced developer to bring your ideas to life, feel free to get in touch.
